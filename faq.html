Everything you need to know about Haul.

The product

What is Haul?

Haul is an iOS app that lets you screenshot any outfit and instantly find where to buy every piece. From the jacket to the shoes — our AI identifies each item and shows you matching products from hundreds of stores.

How does it work?

Screenshot an outfit on Instagram, TikTok, or any app. Share it to Haul. Our AI detects every clothing item, bag, and accessory in the photo and finds matching products you can buy online.

Is Haul free?

Yes, Haul is completely free. No subscription, no hidden costs.

When does Haul launch?

Haul is coming soon to iOS. Join the waitlist on our homepage to get notified when we launch.

Will there be an Android version?

We're starting with iOS. Android is on our roadmap but we don't have a timeline yet.

How to use

How do I scan an outfit?

Take a screenshot of any outfit, then share it to Haul using the iOS share sheet. You can also open the app and upload a photo directly or take one with your camera.

What items can Haul detect?

Tops, jackets, pants, dresses, skirts, shoes, bags, sunglasses, hats, and more. Haul works best with clear photos where the items are visible.

Why didn't Haul find my item?

Very small items, unusual angles, or low-quality screenshots can be harder to identify. Try a clearer photo. You can also manually select an area of the image to search.

Where do the product results come from?

Haul searches across hundreds of online stores to find matching products. Results include direct links so you can compare prices and buy from the store you prefer.

Account & Privacy

Do I need an account?

Yes, a free account is required to save your scan history and favorites. You can sign up with email, Apple, or Google.

Does Haul sell my data?

No. We never sell your personal data. Your screenshots are used only to identify clothing items. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Open Haul, go to Settings, scroll down and tap "Delete Account". This permanently deletes your account and all associated data.
